About Me

Kevin Macaraeg is studying full stack web development at the UC Berkeley Extension. He studied marketing and visual design at San Francisco State University. He is pursuing a career in creating innovative interfaces and disruptive web products.

On the weekends, he plays music with his friends, explores nature and occasionally runs competitive races. He is living the beautiful Sunset District and frequently travels home to San Diego. He loves California and will never leave. Maybe.

Macaraeg is a frequent reader. His most recommended books include Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari, The 4-Hour Work Week by Tim Ferris, and Don't Make Me Think by Steve Krug. Macaraeg prefers reads nonfiction, self-help, and psychology books. He likes to tune into the perspectives of industry leaders and use their knowledge and experience to enhance his life skills.
